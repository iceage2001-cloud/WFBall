Frequently Asked Questions

How often is quiz content updated?

We review and refresh question banks and guides whenever major tournament updates are announced or when users report outdated details.

How do you verify facts?

We rely on official match data, FIFA announcements, and established sports reporting. Report any issue and we will review it.

Do you track personal information?

Quiz score data is primarily stored in local browser storage to preserve your experience. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can users request new quiz topics?

Yes. Send your suggestions through the contact page, including topic category and question difficulty preference.
